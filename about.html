About Wildfire Logger

Wildfire Logger shows information about wildfires in the U.S.

It was built using: jQuery, AJAX, and Bootstrap4

InciWeb (Incident Information Systems) RSS, Google Maps API, and Twitter APIS were used to gather data.

This project was made as Vikki Okamura because she is from California where there are a lot of wildfires, and wanted to build a way to access information easily about wildfires.
